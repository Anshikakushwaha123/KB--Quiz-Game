KBC Rules (English)

  1. You will be shown 10 questions on one page.
  2. Select one option per question. You can change answers before submitting.
  3. Click Submit All to grade — score out of 10 will be shown.
  4. No negative marking; each correct = 1 point.

Quick Info

Your account and progress are stored locally in this browser. You can play multiple batches. To change user, click Logout.
